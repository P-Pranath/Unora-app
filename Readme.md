## What Unora Is

**Unora is a commitment-first connection platform** designed to fix what modern dating and social apps structurally break: meaningful human connection.

Unora introduces intentional friction:
- No instant chat
- No swipe economy
- No photo-first judgment
- No infinite choice

Connection is earned through consistency.

---

## The Core Insight

Modern connection platforms optimize for:
- Speed
- Volume
- Engagement
- Dopamine

Unora was built on a contrarian belief:

> **Anticipation builds attachment.  
Effort creates signal.  
Consistency reveals intent.**

Unora doesn‚Äôt try to win attention.  
It tries to **filter for people who are willing to invest effort**.
