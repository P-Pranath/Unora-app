## 2. Screen Purpose & Context

### 2.1 The Premium Transaction

This modal appears when a user taps on a **locked reveal** they could purchase but haven't yet earned through consistency. It offers immediate access while respecting the "Earned" philosophy by explicitly showing the free alternative.

**Philosophy:**
> "Can't wait? Peek at their [Lifestyle] now."

This is not a dark pattern. The modal:
- Clearly states the price
- Explicitly shows when they'd unlock it for free
- Allows easy dismissal without pressure
- Respects tier purchase caps
